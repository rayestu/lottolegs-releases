# LottoLegs

> **Beta — Android only**

LottoLegs turns your daily steps into coins to play daily games and climb the leaderboard — all powered by how much you walk.

---

## How it works

1. **Walk** — your steps are tracked automatically in the background
2. **Earn coins** — steps convert to coins you can spend in the app
3. **Play daily games** — a new mini-game drops every day; your score ranks you against other players
4. **Leaderboard** — see how your scores stack up against everyone else

---

## Download

Go to the [Releases](https://github.com/rayestu/lottolegs-releases/releases) page and download the latest `.apk` file.

### Install steps

1. Download the `.apk` to your Android device
2. Open the file — you may be prompted to allow installs from unknown sources
3. Tap **Install**
4. Open LottoLegs and create an account

> **Allow installs from unknown sources:** Settings → Apps → Special app access → Install unknown apps → your browser → Allow

---

## Requirements

- Android 8.0 or higher
- Internet connection required

## Beta notes

This is an early beta build. Bugs are expected. If you run into anything broken, report it directly to the developer.

- Do not share your login credentials
